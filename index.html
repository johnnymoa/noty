    About Noty

    Noty saves all your notes locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

    Tips to get started:

    • Click the notebook icon in the top left to toggle the sidebar
    • Your notes are automatically saved as you type
    • Export your notes regularly to keep them safe

    Organize with Bookmarks

    Use your browser's bookmarks to organize notes:

    • Bookmark the current page to save a specific note
